## 1. The Core Idea

> **Truth made legible.**

The playbook turns brand development into a testable, automatable science rooted in cognitive psychology, behavioral economics, and ethical design.  
Every Crankbird product, from the Email Parser MVP to future modules, embodies this principle through three universal mechanisms:

- **Category Entry Points (CEPs):** Make the brand easy to recall in buying moments.  
- **Distinctive Brand Assets (DBAs):** Make it easy to recognize instantly.
