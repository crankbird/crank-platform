## Personas
- **P1 Author‑Curator**: writes fast; needs tidy metadata without thinking about it.
- **P2 Collaborator**: contributes without memorising house style; wants clear, friendly checks.
- **P3 Automation Agent**: depends on predictable metadata; needs machine‑readable reports.

## Design Implications
Explainable rules; soft enforcement; CLI + pre‑commit; NDJSON reports; project overrides.
