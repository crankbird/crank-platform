Concept:

AI can optimise planning, not presence.

Electrical work exists where cognition meets constraint -- embodied intelligence interacting with matter and risk.

As automation accelerates elsewhere, embodied trades become scarcer and more valuable.

The tactile moat is not inefficiency; it's irreplaceable competence.
