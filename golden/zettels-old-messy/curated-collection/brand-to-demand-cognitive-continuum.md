Concept:

Brand is memory; demand is attention.

RevOps unites them by wiring brand sentiment, engagement, and customer data into one adaptive system.

When the same ontology describes why people care and what they buy, marketing becomes a form of cognition -- continuously learning, predicting, and improving.
