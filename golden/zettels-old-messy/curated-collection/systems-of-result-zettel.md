## Concept

Enterprise systems have evolved from passive data stores to interactive platforms to insight engines. The emerging next phase is **Systems of Result** ‚Äî systems that not only interpret data but act on it, often autonomously.

| **System of Engagement** | Interaction           | Who‚Äôs involved?        |

---

## Defining ‚ÄúSystem of Result‚Äù

A **System of Result** is a closed-loop system where:
- Insights are translated into autonomous or semi-autonomous actions
- Outcomes are optimized across constraints (cost, trust, energy, latency, etc.)
- Human involvement may be minimal, supervisory, or policy-level

This represents a shift:
- From **data visibility** to **operational agency**
- From **reporting** to **real-time orchestration**
- From **platforms** to **intent-aware execution**

## Real-World Examples

- FinOps AI rebalancing cloud spend automatically
- Self-healing infrastructure (AIOps, automated rollback, scaling)
- Agent-based supply chain reconfiguration
- Autonomous fraud mitigation
- Real-time experience personalization

## Strategic Implications

- **Architectural Shift**: Requires APIs, policy layers, orchestration engines, and feedback loops.
- **Governance Models**: Outcome accountability, delegated decision rules, compliance tracing.
- **Value Realization**: No longer about building dashboards ‚Äî it's about building systems that *deliver* change.
