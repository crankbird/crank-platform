## Summary
Tulving’s **HERA model** (1994) showed asymmetrical hemispheric activity during encoding and retrieval.
- **Left prefrontal cortex:** semantic encoding.
- **Right prefrontal cortex:** episodic retrieval.

## Evidence Type
PET imaging

## Key Sources
- Tulving et al. (1994). *Hemispheric encoding/retrieval asymmetry in episodic memory.* **PNAS**, 91(6), 2016–2020.
- Nyberg et al. (1996). *Human prefrontal cortex activation during episodic memory retrieval.* **Neuroreport**, 7(15), 2793–2796.

## Reflection
The lateralized architecture supports a dual-channel model of memory. In **Brand Engineering**, this maps to two hemispheres of brand cognition: *rational encoding* (proof structures) vs *experiential recall* (emotional memory).
